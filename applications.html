Industry Applications

Laser welding is used in many sectors because of speed, precision, and automation compatibility. In automotive manufacturing, laser welding is used for body panels, tailored blanks, and battery-related components (Agarwal, 2023).

Automotive

  • Body-in-white seam welding
  • Powertrain and transmission components
  • Electric vehicle battery assemblies

Aerospace

  • Lightweight structure joining
  • Precision welds for engine parts
  • High repeatability for strict quality standards

Medical Devices

  • Small stainless and titanium components
  • Low heat input for sensitive parts
  • Clean surface finish for sterile applications

Electronics

  • Battery tabs and connectors
  • Micro-welding for thin sections
  • Consistent automated production

Key Observation

As industries move toward lightweight design and high-volume automation, laser welding becomes more valuable because it offers both productivity and consistent weld quality (AWS, 2020).
